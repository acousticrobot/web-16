## Waffle Cones

  * 1 whole egg
  * 1 egg white
  * 1⁄4 teaspoon salt
  * 1⁄4 cup granulated sugar
  * 2⁄3 cup sifted all-purpose flour
  * 2 tablespoons butter melted and cooled slightly
  
  * beat eggs and salt
  * add sugar 1 minute
  * incorporate flour and remove lumps
  * Spoon 3 tablespoons (1 1⁄2 ounces) of batter per waffle
