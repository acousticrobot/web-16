New Hope for Caspar
Since this painting is not record of an actual iceberg, but rather a romantic expression, not all of the details of the painting make sense when translated into physical space. Just at Friedrich used artistic license in creating the painting, I had to cheat the angles and shapes of the foam to conform to the contours of the painting. My intention is to render as accurately as possible the shapes and proportions of the painting when viewed from a specific point, while at the same time creating a form that is effective visually from all angles.

R10.4

 2002
colored paper, polystyrene
