Apple Crisp8 apples ( 1/2 granny smith, 1/2 golden delicious )lemon3/4 c. sugar1/2 c. oatmeal1/2 c. flour1 tsp. cinnamon1/3 c. butter, unsaltedspray large rectangular baking dishpeel and slice apples, brush w/ lemon juice, and layer in dishmix remaining until crumblytop apples with mixtureBake 350 for 40 min
