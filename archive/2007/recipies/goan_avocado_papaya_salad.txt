2 avocados, semi-ripe 1/2 medium papaya, not too soft 2-3 limes (~ 1/4 cup juice), plus zest of 1 lime1/2 medium red onion, minced 1 teaspoon ginger, minced 2 heaping tablespoons corn1 jalapeno pepperfresh cilantro1 teaspoon ground cumin1/2 teaspoon ground coriander1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepperSalt and pepperPeel and pit the avocados and slice the flesh into 10 wedges. Peel and seed the papaya and slice the flesh into 12 wedges. Add to avocado.Add zest of one lime.Juice the limes into a non-reactive bowl, add red onion and ginger to bloom for 5 minutes. Spread juice mixture over papaya and avocado.Add minced jalapeno, corn, spices, and lime zest and gently toss. Season with salt and pepper, to taste. Refrigerate for at least 15 minutes to let flavors develop.
