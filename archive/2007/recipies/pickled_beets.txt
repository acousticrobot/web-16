Pickled Beetsversion 1boil 2 lbs beets c. 30 minheat up vinegar sauce:1 cup apple cider vinegar1/4 c sugar [modified 2nd time: 2 TBSP]pulp from 2 tamarind podsspoon grated ginger1 Tsp coriander seedssprig cilantro leavessprig Thai basil3 cardamom podsdrain, peel and slice beetsdrain the vinegar sauce over beetschillversion 2:1 tam podbunch ginger2 seranno peppersturmericstar anisecorianderfresh thymecardamom, 4 podscloveslittle oilcinnamon stick
