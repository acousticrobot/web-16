potato leek carrot soupChop:2 leeks1 medium onion2 cloves garlic1 tbsp gingerand sauté in a large sauce pan in:	3 tbsp olive oilfor 4-5 minutes until onions are translucent. Slice thinly:	1 large potato	4 carrotsand add to pot with:	sprig fresh thyme	couple bay leaves	salt to taste	fresh ground pepper	x-cups veggie stock, or water and veggie bullion	brewer's yeastsimmer 1/2 hour, stirring occasionallylet cool to warm, and pull out bay leaves. Transfer to blender and blend until smooth.Transfer back to stock pot and reheat, adding water if necessary.[source: altered JOC potato leek soup]
