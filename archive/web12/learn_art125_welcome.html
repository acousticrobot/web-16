Welcome to Art125 Topics in Contemporary Art

Hello and welcome to Art125 Topics in Contemporary Art. My name is Jonathan Gabel, and I will be your professor for this class. Along with the course material which will be available on Mercer’s Website I will be maintaining pages here on my own website where we can continue the dialogue throughout the class. Each unit will have it’s own page. I will usually repeat the content from the introduction section here. This is also a good place to post general questions for each unit. These pages will always be grouped together at jonathangabel.com/learn/art125 so go ahead and bookmark it now. The orange and gray circle in the side menu will also always get you there.

Once again, any questions that you have, probably someone else has them too, so don’t be shy, ask away. Each unit will have a page on my website where you can post comments or questions, and I should be able to reply pretty quickly.

Introduce yourself below so I can add you to the list of approved participants, and use this page to ask or post anything related to the class in general.
